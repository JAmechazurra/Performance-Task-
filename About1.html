About

The world was a project created by a group of beginner programmers called "Out of the world gals," consisting
of five 3rd year high school student members, who each have similar ideals and opinions about the current situation
in the year 2020.


What is "The World" Project?

The World Project is a project designed to inform and persuade those people who are affected or not by the Covid-19
Pandemic. Through this webpage, those who are unaware of the deadly pandemic will be able to access and understand
what the "today" of every people back then lived with and how those people were able to stand up for "tomorrow,"
